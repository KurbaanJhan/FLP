Let's Get Straight to Business

Our mission here is to teach financial vocabulary in a colorful way.

Young kids these days are taught math, science, and a bunch of other things that, sure, will help in your line of work and increase your memorization capability, but it doesn't help in life. In life, you must learn the ways of money. The purpose of this website isn't to teach you financing and all that, but only the terminology in a way that looks enjoyable.
